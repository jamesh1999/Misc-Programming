Frequently Asked Questions


How do I reset my handwarmer(s)?

The handwarmers need only be heated in water over 60 degrees for ~10 minutes to reset. However, sometimes they can recrystalise instantly. If this is the case, we have found that taking them out half way through and squishing them up a bit before continuing ensures that they are properly reset.


When will the folepunchers begin shipping?

We have already contacted those who preordered their folepunchers but we don't want to rush production and risk compromising our product. However, it is likely that they will start to be shipped within the next few weeks.
