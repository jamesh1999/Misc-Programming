Young Enterprise

Young Enterprise is a not-for-profit business and enterprise education charity in England & Wales. It was founded in 1962 by Sir Walter Salomon and every year it helps 250,000 young people to set up and run businesses for a year. To this date, 3.8 million people have been helped along the road to becoming entrepreneurs by Young Enterprise. Young Enterprise's mission statement is "to inspire and equip young people to learn and succeed through enterprise."
